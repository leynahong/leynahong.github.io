About Me

Hi I'm Leyna! I'm a junior at Claremont McKenna College studying Economics and Philosophy. It's great to meet you!

I've never really worn jewelry until just recently, and when I did, I found it difficult to find affordable jewelry from brands that I felt like I could trust. I decided to learn more about how to make jewelry and fell in love with the process! With every piece of jewelry I make, there is so much opportunity for creativity, and especially when I make jewelry for others, it's exciting being able to create a unique piece of jewelry to capture people's personalities.

Feel free to email me at leynahong8@gmail.com to collab, share new ideas, or just talk!
