Notes on Products and Prices

I am currently not making necklackes since they are more complicated to make, but I hope to eventually add necklaces to the selection as well!

As you can see, some prices have a plus sign, which indicates that it may end up costing more than the given range. This is because I try to be flexible with the custom orders, so if I accept a more complicated custom order, I may end up charging more than the range given. This range is just what I charge on average.

To place an order, just email me at leynahong8@gmail.com and explain your custom order in as much detail as you can. Pictures as references for what you want will be super helpful as well!
